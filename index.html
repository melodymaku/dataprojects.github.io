HR Analytics | Optimizing Employee Performance & Benefits Through Data-Driven Insights

An interactive Tableau dashboard for Tech Innovators Inc. showing employee performance and benefit utilization across 244 staff in 7 departments. Revealed key drivers of success such as training attendance boosting scores by +2.7%, high engagement aligning with top performers, and strong results in Marketing and IT while surfacing gaps in HR, Legal, and Finance. On the benefits side, 95% of employees used at least one program, with gym memberships and health insurance leading, though satisfaction remained moderate (3.07/5), particularly among younger and older age groups. Designed for executive review, the dashboard empowered HR leaders with clear insights and actionable recommendations to expand training, strengthen engagement, support underperforming teams, and tailor benefits more inclusively.

Performance Metrics + Sales Trends | Choco De Luxe

This interactive Tableau dashboard visualizes key sales insights for Choco de Luxe, a premium European chocolate brand. It showcases performance across locations, channels, products, and sales personnel, enabling strategic decisions around pricing, delivery, and product mix. With over €5.13M in revenue and a 46% profit margin, the dashboard highlights high-performing cities, top-selling products, and digital channel effectiveness—while surfacing opportunities to reduce cancellations and elevate underperforming regions and team members. Designed for executive review, the dashboard blends clarity with depth, empowering data-driven growth across the business.
